# Terminus

**Type a place. Watch a poster build itself.**

Terminus turns any city, address, or landmark into a stunning, fully customizable map poster — live in your browser, no account required, free to download.

---

## What it does

Search for anywhere in the world and Terminus instantly renders a beautiful map poster centered on that location. From there, make it yours:

- **Toggle map layers** — show or hide roads, highways, water, parks, buildings, railways, and labels
- **Choose a style preset** — Minimal, Noir, Blueprint, Terrain, Neon, Vintage, Monochrome, or start from scratch
- **Customize typography** — add a headline (the place name), coordinates, or your own text; pick from curated fonts, sizes, and positions
- **Pick your format** — A3 to 24×36", portrait or landscape, with border options and shape masks (rectangle, circle, hexagon)
- **Reposition freely** — drag to pan, scroll to zoom, frame exactly the view you want

Every change renders in real time. What you see is exactly what you get.

## Why Terminus

Every other custom map poster tool makes you pay before you can see what you're actually getting. Terminus flips that: design first, pay only if you want something physical. The editor runs entirely in your browser using WebGL, so edits are instant — no waiting on server renders, no "processing your preview" spinners.
